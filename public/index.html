55Prophets


55Prophets is a fantasy betting app where you can place bets on anything.

Want to bet on who will be the first pick in the NFL draft? Go for it. Want to bet on who Don Draper will verbally abuse next on Mad Men? Look out Pete.

You can create and join leagues, just like fantasy football, about all sorts of different topics such as sports, television shows, and politics.

In each league, you start with 10,000 prophits (Ƥ), which is our fantasy currency. You can then create questions of any kind and place bets them.

The best part? It’s competitive.

You’re competing against the other members of the league to accumulate the biggest cash pile. Now, your friend who acts like he knows everything will have to put his (virtual) money where his mouth is.

Keep up with what's happening in your leagues with a continuous activity stream.

See who's making bets, what questions are published, and who got the latest big payout.

55Prophets is being designed and built in Chicago, IL by the Federis Group.
